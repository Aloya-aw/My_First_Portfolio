Swimming and Me

How It All Begun

There was a period in the university where I got mugged and hddd my phone taken away from me by a guy wearing a mask and carrying a machete. I guess from the shock of it all and my gratitude for making it out alive, I didn't care too much that the phone was gone.

Besides, I wasnt exactly expecting anyone to etrying to reach me. The next morning, bored and with nothing to do, I went to the university poolside with a friend, and that's how I met the gentleman who became my swimming instructor.

How It Played Out

We had a very good friendship and after a year, he decided to teach me for free. I started swimming without help by the 3rd day.

First Milestone

My first milestone was when I ws able to hit 20 laps of 17.5m without stopping for more than 10 secs to rest.

Second Milestone

My second milestone was when I was able to go a full 50m strech all the way to the 21ft end of the swimming pool.

Where I Am Now

I'm not a competitive swimmer and its been quite a while since I found myself in a pool, but any chance to swim again, I would absolutely jump at it.
